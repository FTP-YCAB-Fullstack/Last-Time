Guiding the Public on how to properly be sustainable one trash at a time.

The web application uses enterprise class AI algorithms to classify types of trash to its appropriate category for disposal. The technology can be implemented on trashcans to guide people put trash in the right place.

Features

An intuitive implementation of crucial information necessary for saving the world and mantaining sanity.

Real Time Imaging

The camera fires immediately and data is captured, analyzed and the results analyzed in real-time before your eyes. The live video feed can be sent over Bluetooth or connected over wifi

AI trained data

The technology uses AI technology to classify the images to their respective categories. The AI model is a multi-label classifier that uses the Google Cloud technology to relay data over the web.

Easy to use and implement

The app can be accessed directly from an IoT embedded device or through the link provided with the trained model. Webcams of any size can be used. It is a plug and play machine.

Easy to understand

The app has a single start button and everything is automatically done in the background. The UI is user experience oriented to suit people of all ages. It does not matter your knowledge background on waste disposal.

Over 90% accuracy

The testing dataset had an 93% accuracy when compared to the training data as it is founded upon quality data. The accuracy was tested over a wide range of backgrounds and objects both opened and deformed.

Available in many platforms

The app can run as a web app, an android or iOS app or or IoT implemented devices such as microcontrollers. Currenlty, only a web app is in place but multisystem and scalability capabilities are feasible.
